Never Miss Another Adventure

Published on January 18, 2017.

While this is not an adventure of the nautical kind, I’ve just had an adventure adding more dynamic content to a static content only blog. If this doesn’t make sense to you don’t worry about it.

The important thing is that there’s now a way for you to receive our adventures directly via email. While this is not my personal preference I’ve had a few readers ask for this feature. Not wanting to disappoint I quickly hacked something together to make it happen.

If you’re one of the readers wanting to receive emails with our adventures, type your email address in the box below and subscribe to our mailing list.
